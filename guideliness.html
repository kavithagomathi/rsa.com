    Manuscript preparation

    Authors have to submit their manuscripts in the IJIRST paper format only. Submission must include title, authors details with affiliations and email id, abstract with keywords. Kindly mention the corresponding author.

    • Submit your manuscript to: editorinchief@ijirst.in
    • Download Copy Rights form. Fill the details and resend the scanned copy to editorinchief@ijirst.in

    Notification of acceptance

    Once article submitted, within 24 hrs acceptance notification will acknowledge to the corresponding author with review results after completion of review process. In case of any revision have to made by the author, it will be done and resubmit along with filled and scanned copyright form.

    Publication of Manuscripts

    Accepted manuscripts are published within 3 days after acceptance provided.

    Best Paper Awards

    The editorial committee would select the best paper from every issue and E- Certificate would be given to the authors of selected manuscript as Best Paper Award.

    Publication Certificates

    Authors of published papers would be provided with E- Certificate to all the authors. Hard copy of the authors will be send based on the request with additional charges.
