    Aim & Scope

    International Journal of Innovative Research in Science & Technologies (IJIRST) aims to enhance the research ideas of the students, scholars, faculty members and industrialists by exchanging the knowledge in the form of articles in new emerging trends which required enormous focus and exposure on latest research. To strengthen the research knowledge of upcoming students and research scholars, IJIRST will act as gateway to access the high-quality research article with free of cost.
