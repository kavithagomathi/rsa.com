    Who is RS Research Academy?

    RS Research Academy is broad Research & Development (R&D) service provider since 2019 in Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, India. RSRA is registered academy under the Ministry of MSME, Government of India. Also, a registered member of National Career Service (NCS) for providing Skill Development Courses to the Undergraduate (UG), Postgraduation (PG) students.

    Signed MoU with various educational institutions to support R&D services. Organized International conferences, National level Webinars, Certification courses, Thesis/ Research article writing services, Journal publication provider (SCOPUS, WoS, SCI), etc., is the major service offering successfully. RSRA initiated an individual unit named "Eagle Publisher" as Journal & Book publisher to publish the unpublished Research articles and Books.
